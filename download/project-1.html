UCI Verminlingua (Anteater) Express

An internal application developed for UC Irvine's Anteater Express (campus bus system) to facilitate the process of scheduling and managing shuttles, shuttle routes and employees.
Responsible for: Routes, buses/shuttles, sessions management and scheduling. Live Field Status and Master Schedule pages

Category : Full Stack Web Application
Tech Stack : HTML, CSS, React, JavaScript, PHP and MySQL
Client : University of California Irvine
Completion : January 2020
Role : Full Stack Engineer
